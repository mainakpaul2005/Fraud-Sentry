About Us

We are committed to tracking and analyzing fraudulent activity to keep the public informed and safe.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide clear, easy-to-understand information to help you stay safe online. We created this website to give back by sharing practical tips, real-life examples, and simple guidance you can trust. Whether you're new to the digital world or looking to strengthen your online security, we’re here to help you recognize, avoid, and defend against threats — making your internet experience safer and more confident.

Our Team

We are a group of friends united by a common purpose — to raise awareness about digital fraud among individuals aged 40 to 70. As online services continue to grow rapidly, we noticed that many people in this age group are becoming frequent targets of scams. Through this website, we aim to share knowledge, tips, and real-world examples to help protect and empower them in navigating the digital world safely.

Join Our Community

Access your account to view personalized insights, tips, and data focused on fraud prevention. Stay informed with updates tailored to your needs, helping you recognize threats early and take proactive steps to protect yourself in today’s digital world.
